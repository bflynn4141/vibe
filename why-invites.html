Why Invites?

We're building a culture, not just a product.

The first 100 members set the tone for everyone who comes next. That's not gatekeeping. That's gardening.

The Short Version

Every party starts small. The people who arrive first shape the vibe for everyone who follows. We're not exclusive because we think we're better. We're small on purpose because we want to get the culture right before we scale.

How It Works

  • Every member gets 3 guest passes
  • When someone redeems yours, you earn another
  • There's no application. No approval process.
  • Just a friend saying: "Hey, I think you'd vibe here."

"The invitation should feel like a gift from a friend, not approval from a committee."

The Terminal Party

Think of /vibe as a party, not a club. Everyone's invited — you just need to know someone who's already there. Our founding members aren't bouncers. They're hosts.

Their job isn't to keep people out. It's to make sure everyone who arrives feels like they belong.

Our Commitment

  • Everyone in the first 1,000 will always be recognized
  • We will never sell your data
  • We will never add ads
  • We will never optimize for "engagement"
  • We will open up — just not yet

On Deck

If genesis is full, you're in the next wave. Not second class — just next. We're building in cohorts because each wave learns from the last.

This isn't a line. It's a green room.
